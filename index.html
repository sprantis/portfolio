About Me

My name is Sarah Prantis and I graduated from UNH with a BA in Studio Art in 2018. Since graduating, I have taken on many roles from receptionist for HIPPA related fields, Service coordinator for individuals with disabilities, and work in a call-center for internal IT services for a corporate company. All these careers have one thing in common; helping people. I worked with and for a large groups of people needing resources pulled together to help them to be able to create, build, manage, and succeed in their lives.I have 15 years of customer service experience that have taught me I have a passion for helping people directly and indirectly to get their needs and wants met. Currently, I am merging my passion for technology and helping people with becoming a Software Developer. I believe it is important that all technologies are adaptable for all individuals needs and skill sets.
