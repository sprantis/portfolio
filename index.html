About Me

My name is Sarah Prantis and I graduated from UNH with a BA in Studio Art in 2018. After graduation, I've worked in a variety of roles spanning from medical receptionist to corporate IT Support. All these careers have allowed me exercise an ongoing goal of mine; helping people. I've worked with people from very different walks of life, but the one thing that remained constant was the need for resources to be accessible so that they can succeed in their goals. With my 15 years of customer service experience, I know that my passion is to help people meet their goals and better their lives. Currently, I am merging that passion and my experience in technology through software development. My biggest interest is accessibility through meaningful design, because I believe that technology is an amazing tool that everyone has the right to leverage.
